# Review Checklist

Use this checklist before moving any task to Ready for Approval.

## Product Integrity
- customer-facing wording unchanged unless explicitly approved
- quote behavior unchanged unless explicitly approved
- no silent scope expansion

## Architecture Integrity
- structured data remains source of truth
- Google Sheet remains renderer adapter only
- no architecture drift from approved docs

## Privacy Integrity
- no unnecessary identity data in prompts
- trust zones respected
- no unsafe exposure of customer information

## Delivery Integrity
- task packet requirements addressed
- deliverables completed
- verification criteria satisfied
- risks documented

## PR Integrity
- summary is clear
- files changed are understandable
- reviewer can see why change was made
